About this site

Privacy Notice

This website collects no personal data, uses no cookies, and includes no tracking or advertising. Here is exactly how it works.

Home › Privacy Notice

This site exists to share the findings of the Woodland Academy Trust AI Impact Report. It is a set of plain, static web pages, designed to respect your privacy by default.

What we collect

Nothing. There are no accounts, no sign-in, no forms and no comment fields. The site does not use cookies, and it contains no analytics, advertising, tracking pixels or third-party scripts. We do not collect, store or share any personal data about you.

No external requests

Everything the site needs — the fonts, the charting library, the logo and images — is served from this website itself. The pages make no calls to third-party services, so simply reading the site does not share your information with anyone else.

Your reading-controls preferences

The accessibility "Reading controls" (text size, high-contrast mode and extra spacing) save your chosen settings in your browser's local storage — on your own device only. These preferences never leave your device and are not transmitted to us or anyone else. You can remove them at any time using the Reset all button in the controls, or by clearing your browser's site data.

"Listen to this page"

The listen-aloud feature uses the speech synthesiser built into your own web browser or device to read the page text aloud. The text is processed on your device; this site does not send anything to an external server to provide this feature.

Hosting and server logs

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages (GitHub, Inc.). As with any web server, GitHub may automatically record standard technical information — such as your IP address and browser type — in its server logs for security and operational purposes. This is handled by GitHub, not by this site. You can read more in the GitHub Privacy Statement and GitHub's information on GitHub Pages data collection.

In short

  • No cookies, no analytics, no advertising, no trackers.
  • No personal data collected, stored or shared by this site.
  • No third-party requests — fonts, charts and images are served locally.
  • Reading-controls preferences stay in your browser, on your device only.
  • "Listen aloud" runs on your device using your browser's built-in speech.

Children's privacy

This site is intended for education professionals and the wider public. Because it collects no personal data from anyone, it collects none from children either.

Changes and contact

If the site's approach to privacy changes, this notice will be updated. For any questions about the report or this notice, please contact Woodland Academy Trust, whose AI initiative was undertaken in partnership with Mark Anderson (ICT Evangelist).

Last updated: September 2025.
